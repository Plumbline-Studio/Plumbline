01 — The idea

One standard, four ways to deliver it.

I take an overlooked or constrained party — an artisan skimmed by middlemen, a practice boxed in by geography, a person locked out by technical skill — and build them the leverage to grow on their own terms. Every tool is held to one test: does it make an unfair or limited equation more true?
